## ElevenLabs 一整段稿（复制用）

**EN**

> This is Yakushima Bus — a free guide to buses and ferries on Yakushima Island. Schedules and fares are compiled from the island tourism association and local transport operators, and updated when those official sources change. The interface and stop search work in Japanese, Chinese, and English — the same names you see at the bus stop.
>
> On the timetable page, pick a route or tap a preset. See the next departure, a live countdown, and Japan time. Browse the full day — weekday, Saturday, or holidays — with two operators color-coded.
>
> On Routes and fares, open the official route map and look up an adult one-way fare between two stops. On Yakushima buses you pay when you get off: take a numbered boarding ticket — a seiriken — when you board; your fare depends on where you board and alight. Our prices are for reference; check the official materials linked below and what the driver charges. For visitors, we also list the island unlimited-ride passes — one, three, and four days — plus where you can buy them.
>
> Getting to and from the island: we list both jetfoil and conventional ferry timetables and fares from Kagoshima. Jetfoil tickets can often be booked online; other operators’ official links are at the bottom of the page.
>
> All the details and source links are on yakushimabus.com. Have a great trip on Yakushima.

**ZH 字幕（建议 8–10 条，与上表一致）**

1. 这是 Yakushima Bus：屋久岛公交与船班查询工具。  
2. 时刻与运价整理自岛上游协与当地交通官方发布，并随官方更新同步。  
3. 界面与站名支持日、中、英，与站牌一致。  
4. 选区间或常用预设；下一班、倒计时、日本时间。  
5. 全天班次：平日 / 周六 / 假日，两家运营商分色。  
6. 官方路线图与两站参考票价；上车取整理券，下车按区间付费，票价仅供参考。  
7. 列出 1 / 3 / 4 日岛内无限乘坐乘车券及购票处。  
8. 鹿儿岛↔屋久岛：高速船与渡轮时刻及票价；高速船可网上预约，更多链接在页底。  
9. 详情见 yakushimabus.com。祝你在屋久岛旅途顺利。
